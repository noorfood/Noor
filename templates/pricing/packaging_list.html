💡 How this works
  • Empty Sack: Recorded as an expense for every 10kg sack packaged.
  • Nylon Piece: Recorded as 10 units of expense for every 10kg sack packaged (assuming 10kg sack holds 10 smaller units if applicable, or just per nylon used).
  • Cleaning Fee: Recorded as an expense for every 100kg bag of raw material issued to cleaners.
  • General Labour: Automatically calculated as a cost for every 10kg sack sold (both SM and Direct sales).
